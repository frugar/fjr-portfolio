While managing the NECA/WizKids digital sculpting department, I managed and directed all of digital sculpting for the HeroClix game, producing nearly 6,000 figures over an eight year period. Recruiting and mentoring a team of sculptors from around the world, I was responsible for guiding all of the digital sculptures from conceptualization into production and assuring high quality representations of licensor brands. Over 750 million HeroClix miniature figures have been sold worldwide.



Every once in a while, but not often enough,I would find some time to sculpt a figure or two myself. In the above image, I sculpted the Supreme Intelligence, the smokey demons effect for Scarlet Witch figure, the "bomb stand" for the Hawkgirl figure, and the flock of bats featured inthe Man-Bat sculpture.
